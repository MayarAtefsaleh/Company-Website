About us

Who are we

The expertise and diversity of the firm founders have helped to create harmony and strong bonds in its management’s leadership, by adding value due to their knowledge in Saudi Arabia, for the 10 years working in high profile, multi‐million Saudi riyal projects in the kingdom. Their proficiency has also amplified by collaborating with international and great reputable Contractors of the likes of Consultants and Project Management Firms such as Saudi Bin Laden Group, Proger, Dar Al Handasa , Arup , Shaker, etc. elevates the profile of the firm to compete with the other reputable established companies in the field. Al‐Badeel has been specialized and has distinguished itself in Reinforced Concrete, finishing, Landscaping, Mechanical, Electrical,Plumbing, Civil defence and HVAC works in the Construction Industry Services (CIS) utilizing all the engineering services related to itsproduction, thus becoming distinguished pioneers in this sector in the Saudi market.

MISSION
Al‐Badeel is Full Service Engineering and Contracting Firm, specialized in concrete MEP works execution and serving clients in all areas in the workflow. The firm focuses in providing the highest quality and standards within a minimal time frame, a rarity with contractors in the field, yet we strive to achieve this goal. Our purpose to serve our clients so that their expectations for their projects are met and even exceeded. Our most important service, outpost is happy, 100% satisfied customer.
